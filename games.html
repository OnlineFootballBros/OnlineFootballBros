Frequently Asked Questions

We've collected some frequently asked questions from users, hoping to help you better understand Football Bros

Football Bros uses advanced recommendation algorithms based on your browsing history, game preferences, and feedback from other users to recommend games. Our system continuously learns and optimizes to provide the most accurate game recommendations. Additionally, our team of game experts regularly curates featured game collections to ensure you discover high-quality game content.

You can use the search function at the top of our website to directly search for game names or keywords. Additionally, we provide multiple categorization methods allowing you to filter by game type, platform, release year, and more. On the game category page, you can use the filters on the left to precisely locate the game types you're interested in.

Our game database is updated daily to ensure the latest game information is provided. Newly released games are typically added to our database on the day of release or the following day. Game ratings, reviews, and user feedback are updated in real-time. Our editorial team also regularly reviews and updates game descriptions and detailed information to ensure accuracy.

You only need to register a Football Bros account to join our community. After registration, you can participate in game discussions, share your gaming experiences, post comments and ratings, and interact with other gaming enthusiasts. We also have dedicated community forums and Discord servers, which you can access via the 'Community' link at the top of the website.

Football Bros is primarily a game navigation and information platform; we do not directly provide game download services. However, we provide official purchase or download links for each game, directing you to the game developer's or authorized distributor's website for purchase or download. This ensures you get legitimate games and supports the work of game developers.

If you are a game developer, you can submit your game through the 'Developer Resources' link at the bottom of our website. You will need to provide basic information about your game, screenshots, videos, system requirements, and other detailed materials. Our review team will review it within 5 business days of receiving the submission and add your game to our database after approval.
